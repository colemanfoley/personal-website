Playlist Me

This was my personal project at Hack Reactor. It's basically Quora for music recommendations. You ask for music recommendations on a certain theme, then others can search for songs to add, getting back search results from Rdio. Then, when you browse answers to questions, you can play the songs through the Rdio API.
